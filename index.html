About Me

Hello! I’m Annie Gilchrist, Product Manager at high-growth legal tech start-up Lexoo.

A flexible and technical all-rounder, I’m responsible for strategic product decisions, product management and design. I also sub in as a developer, and love getting sucked into code.

In a previous life I worked as a lawyer for global law firm Freshfields Bruckhaus Deringer LLP. I left when I realised why the law didn’t fulfil me – because I love to create and build things.

About Me

Hello! I’m Annie Gilchrist, Product Manager at embedded finance scale–up Currencycloud.

At CC, I’ve been lucky to play a part in our hyper-growth journey – watching us double our valuation and headcount in 18 months before being acquired by Visa. It’s been busy and challenging, and the most amazing experience with an exceptional bunch of people.

A flexible and creative product geek, I love all things fintech - whether leading teams to deliver new products, speaking with prospects and understanding what makes them tick, or learning about the coolest new trends and developments in the market.

In a previous life I worked as a lawyer for global law firm Freshfields Bruckhaus Deringer LLP. I left when I realised why the law didn’t fulfil me: because I love to create and build things.
